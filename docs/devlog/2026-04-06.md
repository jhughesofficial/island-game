# Devlog — 2026-04-06

## Shipped

- **Settings → Main Menu button** — wired `MainMenuBtn` in `settings_panel.gd`; routes to `MainMenu.tscn`
- **VIP/Upgrade button click fix** — debounced panel rebuilds via availability signature (`_get_sig()`); panels only rebuild when the available set actually changes, not on every passive-income frame tick. Eliminates nodes being destroyed mid-click.
- **Click value scaling** — `click_value = max($1, IPS × 0.1) × click_upgrade_mult × VIP_mult`. Owning venues now meaningfully increases what each party click earns.
- **Staff tab** — new tab (between Venues and Upgrades) with 4 auto-clicker staff types. Staff auto-accumulate fractional clicks in `_auto_click_acc` and fire `_add_money(_click_value)` each whole click. Hire multiples, costs scale with `cost_growth` exponent.
  - The Intern ($50 base, 0.5 clicks/s)
  - The Fixer ($750 base, 2 clicks/s)
  - The Coordinator ($8k base, 8 clicks/s)
  - The Handler ($80k base, 30 clicks/s)
- **Purchase multiplier** — ×1/×10/×100/MAX toggle on Venues and Staff tabs. Active option highlighted gold. MAX computes max affordable via loop and buys in one press. `_clear_list()` skips the persistent `MultRow` node.
- **GameState additions**: `staff_counts`, `staff_count_changed` signal, `buy_staff`/`buy_staff_n`/`staff_max_affordable`/`staff_cost`/`staff_cost_n`, `buy_venue_n`/`venue_max_affordable`/`venue_cost_n`, `_tick_auto_click`, staff save/load/reset.

## Design / Decisions

- Click value tied to passive income (10% of IPS) rather than a fixed base — means the player feels building venues directly on every click, not just idle earnings. Upgrades still stack as multipliers on top.
- Staff auto-clicks call `_add_money(_click_value)` directly (not `click_party()`) to avoid spawning floating labels for every auto-click — those would be visually spammy.
- Purchase multiplier stored per-panel (not global) so Venues and Staff can be on different multipliers independently.
